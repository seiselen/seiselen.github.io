QAD Overview of 'Project Broadgate'

Project Broadgate involves, frankly: showcasing and chronicling my coding, design, and otherwise work; alongside enlightening via any insights provided by these artifacts. That is, generally speaking:

  • One part exhibition via live, ideally interactive public demos (i.e. as the entries on the 'Coding Projects' page on this site, videos on my YouTube Channel, 3D models on my SketchFab gallery, etc. serve to accomplish); and
  • One part providing source code and/or other [multi-media] documentation thereof (as the code repos, my YouTube channel, SketchFab model descriptions, and other documentary/publication serves to accomplish)
  • One part providing insight and empowerment to others; for example: to someone who stumbles across my Quadtree demo and gains a better understanding of how they behave and how one could be implemented (as happened in my days TA'ing CSC-455).
